The city that never sleeps

St. Mary's ISCE

St. Mary's School ISCE is where I spent 11 years of my childhood. Being an all boys school, it was immense fun. Our teacherr we're kind, but sometime strict and I owe it to them.
We had churches in school, which was fun.
Located in Mazgaon, the school taught me descipline, gave me the best of education and helped me because a stronger individual. I can easily say that one of the best part of my life is my school career.
