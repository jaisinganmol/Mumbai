Mumbai's Fast Food

Having over 18 million people, this page gives a description on the ready to pick and go street food.

Vada-Pav

An easy-to-make, consisting of casserole, veggie patty, and condiments, this is the fast fast food of Mumbai. My personal favorite.
It cost $0.3 or about 15 rupees. Personally, I have one daily and dearly miss it.

Sev Puri

The picture on the left is another easily made food. Costing about $0.5, it is made with tamarind sauce, sev, coriander, and potato.
Being a special delicacy in India, it is my mum's favorite.
