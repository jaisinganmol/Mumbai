Temples

Haji Ali Dargah

A place located in the middle of the Arabian Sea, Haji Ali is a holy place for the Muslims to pray. It is 20 mins by walk from home. I've often clicked pictures of it while going to work.
During the holy month of Ramzan, Haji Ali does become like Time Square, with not an inch space to move.

Mount Mary Church

Also known as Basilica of Our Lady of the Mount, this church was built in 1640 by the Portugese. Located in Bandra, its known for the Bloody Mary festival.
I often go here with my father and his friends for Sunday mass.
