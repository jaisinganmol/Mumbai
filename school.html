My Schooling

A little bit more where I studied in Mumbai!

St. Mary's School ISCE

My school, St. Mary's School, located at Mazgaon, is where I spent 11 years of my life. My school has two grounds, an auditorium, and a massive church.
In the ten years, the principal has changed 3 times. We kindly address him as 'POP'. St. Mary's School has been an integral part of my career. I have made some wonderful friends for life.
