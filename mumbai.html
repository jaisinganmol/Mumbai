Mumbai's Heritage

Learning Mumbai's rich and cultured heritage

The Iconic Times of India Building

The Times of India building is a landmark building built by the British. This building prints over 18 millions copies of the Times of India news paper.
Located opposite the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, a usual commuter picks his daily paper and commutes for work.

Gateway of India

Built at a Apollo Bunder, the Gateway of India is another monument build by the British that celebrate King George and Queen Mary's arrival to India.
Often while meeting friends, we go to the Gateway of India to click pictures.
