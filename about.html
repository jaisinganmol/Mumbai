City Skyline

Mumbai city is the financial capital of India. It is often said the wealth present is depicted by the colors in the skyline.
The skyline displays the natural beauty, that reflects the ray of hope for every person coming into this big city from small cities.
The Mumbai skyline also depicts the Queens necklace.

Slums

The slums are an essential part to Mumbai development.
With low paying labor, people in the slums help flourish the textile industry and the recycle industry.
In a school trip to the slums, the unfortunate part comes to see poverty that thrives in these slums.
