Transportation

Mumbai is home to more that 18 million people. Local Transportation is the best way, people used to get to and fro from places. I being one of them.

Churchgate Trains

Located in South Bombay, this railway station has 3 station gates. Like me, there are numerous commuters, who use of Churcgate to go to school or work daily.
Unlike most railway stations, Churchgate surprisingly is not very crowded.
Churchgate does connect many others station. There were famous eating joints at Churchgate, which made one reason to go there during my junior college time.

Vasai Train

Vasai station is "THE" of Mumbai. No doubt, with the left, you can see it is the most crowded station in Mumbai.
You do not need to get down, you'll be pushed off. It is as simple as that.
Even I would not dare to board this train.
